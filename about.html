ABOUT US

Banaful Social Welfare Organization (BSWO) was established in 1986. This NGO is a non-profit, non-political and non-partisan, and women-led organization. It has a large healthcare program. In 1997, it started a microfinance program in Chittagong City Corporation and the program now serves about 13,000 members. BSWO uses the traditional rural model and has 99% payment efficiency and more than 70% of its members are women. In addition to these programs, the community is served by BSWO's education, environment, and social movement programs.


Dreams, Missions and Goals

Dream

We dream of a society where all the men and women of our future generations are living with dignity in a legal, social and economic environment free from all forms of discrimination.

Mission

To conduct various activities in an institutional manner based on the organization's own capabilities, skills, experience, and available financial and technical assistance among the target population and based on the needs of the target population.

Goal

Banaful is a social welfare organization. The ultimate goal of this organization is to improve the living standards of backward, disadvantaged, and poor people, especially children and women.

Our Purpose

  The aim is to provide assistance in raising the awareness and knowledge of the people

  The goal is to play a favorable role in the economic development of the people

  The aim is to provide training and technical assistance for community empowerment and leadership development

  The goal is to organize the population and assist in acquiring professional skills in income generation projects
